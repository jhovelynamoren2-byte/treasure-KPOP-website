💎 TREASURE 💎

History and Background

TREASURE is a South Korean boy group formed by YG Entertainment, one of the biggest and most influential entertainment companies in South Korea. The group was created through a survival reality show called "YG Treasure Box," which aired from November 2018 to January 2019. The program featured a large number of male trainees competing against each other in singing, dancing, and performance evaluations. The goal of the show was to select the best trainees who would debut as part of a new boy group under YG Entertainment.

Initially, YG Entertainment planned to debut a 7-member group called TREASURE. However, due to the high level of talent, popularity, and strong fan support for many trainees, the company decided to expand the lineup. A second group called MAGNUM was also announced, consisting of additional selected trainees. Eventually, YG decided to merge both groups into one, forming a 12-member boy group known as TREASURE. This decision made TREASURE one of the largest boy groups under YG Entertainment.

After the survival show ended, the group went through a long preparation period before their official debut. During this time, the members trained intensively in singing, dancing, language skills, and stage performance. They also built a strong connection with fans through social media content, performances, and pre-debut videos. Despite facing delays and challenges, the group continued to improve and prepare for their debut.

TREASURE officially debuted on August 7, 2020, with their single album titled "The First Step: Chapter One." Their debut title track "BOY" showcased their powerful energy, synchronized choreography, and youthful concept. The group quickly gained attention both in South Korea and internationally, especially among K-pop fans. Their debut was considered highly successful, as they broke several records for rookie groups and achieved high album sales.

Following their debut, TREASURE released multiple albums under "The First Step" series, including Chapter Two, Chapter Three, and their first full-length album "TREASURE EFFECT." Each release showed their growth as artists, both musically and performance-wise. Their songs gained popularity worldwide, and they built a strong global fanbase known as "Treasure Maker" or "Teume."

In 2022, TREASURE made a strong comeback with "THE SECOND STEP: CHAPTER ONE," featuring the hit title track "JIKJIN." This era showed a more mature and powerful image compared to their earlier youthful concept. One of their B-side tracks, "DARARI," became a viral hit on social media platforms, especially on TikTok, further increasing their global recognition.

In 2023, the group released their second full-length album titled "REBOOT," marking a new chapter in their career. This album highlighted their growth not only as performers but also as musicians, as many members participated in writing and producing songs. The title track "BONA BONA" demonstrated a stronger, more confident concept, proving their versatility and artistic development.

Aside from their music releases, TREASURE has also been active in concerts and world tours. They successfully held their first concert tour titled "HELLO TOUR," performing in various countries across Asia and beyond. Their performances showcased their live vocals, powerful choreography, and strong stage presence. They later continued with the "REBOOT TOUR," including large-scale concerts and dome performances in Japan, which further solidified their status as global K-pop artists.

Today, TREASURE is recognized as one of the leading boy groups of the fourth generation of K-pop. They are known for their energetic music style, strong teamwork, and active participation in creating their own music. With their continuous growth, talent, and dedication, TREASURE continues to gain more fans worldwide and strengthen their position in the global music industry.


Members Profile


Choi Hyun Suk (최현석): Born on April 21, 1999, in Daegu, South Korea. He serves as the leader, main rapper, and dancer of TREASURE. Hyun-suk is one of the longest trainees in YG Entertainment, which helped him develop strong leadership and performance skills. He is highly respected by the members for guiding the group both on and off stage. Aside from performing, he actively participates in songwriting and composing, contributing to the group’s musical identity. He is also known for his bold fashion sense and confidence.

Park Ji Hoon (박지훈): Born on March 14, 2000, in Busan, South Korea. He is the co-leader, main vocalist, and lead dancer of the group. Jihoon is known for his strong vocals, stable live performances, and precise dancing. He is often referred to as the “mother” of TREASURE because he takes care of the members and ensures their well-being. His long training period reflects his dedication and patience, and he plays an important role in maintaining the group’s teamwork and discipline.

Kanemoto Yoshinori (金本 芳典 / 요시노리): Born on May 15, 2000, in Kobe, Japan. His full name is Kanemoto Yoshinori. He is a rapper, dancer, and lyricist. Yoshi is known for writing meaningful and emotional rap verses, often expressing his personal thoughts and feelings through music. He has a calm and quiet personality off stage, but transforms into a powerful performer on stage. His unique voice and musicality add depth to TREASURE’s songs.

Kim JunKyu (김준규): Born on September 9, 2000, in Chungju, South Korea. He is a main vocalist known for his soft, emotional, and expressive voice. Junkyu trained for nearly 7 years, which shows his dedication and perseverance. He is also known for his kind and gentle personality, making him one of the most lovable members. His vocals often give emotional depth to the group’s songs.

Hamada Asahi (浜田 朝光 / 아사히): Born on August 20, 2001, in Osaka, Japan. His real name is Hamada Asahi. He is a vocalist and one of the producers in the group. Asahi is recognized for his unique vocal tone and artistic creativity. He enjoys painting and composing music, and he has contributed to several songs of TREASURE. His quiet and mysterious personality makes him stand out among the members.

Yoon Jae Hyuk (윤재혁): Born on July 23, 2001, in Yongin, South Korea. He is a vocalist and rapper. Jae-hyuk is known for his visuals and hardworking nature. Despite having a shorter training period compared to other members, he showed rapid improvement and strong determination. His positive attitude and effort make him an important part of the group.

Kim Do Young (김도영): Born on December 4, 2003, in Seoul, South Korea. He is a main dancer and vocalist. Doyoung is known for his clean, sharp, and energetic dance style. He is also very playful and brings fun energy to the group. His dancing skills contribute greatly to TREASURE’s performances.

Park Jeong Woo (박정우): Born on September 28, 2004, in South Korea. He is a main vocalist recognized for his powerful and emotional singing voice. Jeongwoo has a bright and humorous personality, often making the group more lively. His strong vocals play a major role in TREASURE’s music, especially in high notes and emotional parts.

Watanabe Haruto (渡辺 温斗 / 하루토): Born on April 5, 2004, in Fukuoka, Japan. His full name is Watanabe Haruto. He is the main rapper and one of the visual members of the group. Haruto gained popularity even before debut because of his deep voice, tall height, and model-like appearance. On stage, he shows strong charisma and confidence, while off stage, he has a more shy and playful personality.

So Jung Hwan (소정환): Born on February 18, 2005, in Iksan, South Korea. He is the maknae (youngest member), dancer, and vocalist. Jung-hwan has a background in Taekwondo, which helps him perform powerful and controlled dance movements. Despite his young age, he shows professionalism and confidence on stage, proving his talent and potential.


Discography and Albums


TREASURE has built a strong and impressive discography since their debut in 2020. Their music releases showcase their growth as artists, from youthful and energetic concepts to more mature and powerful styles. Their debut began with a series of single albums known as "The First Step" series.

The First Step Series (2020–2021): This series includes "Chapter One," "Chapter Two," and "Chapter Three," which were later compiled into their first full-length album titled "TREASURE EFFECT." Their debut song "BOY" gained massive attention for its strong beat and performance. Other title tracks such as "I LOVE YOU," "MMM," and "MY TREASURE" showed different sides of the group, from powerful to bright and emotional concepts. This series broke records and became one of the most successful debut projects in K-pop.

The Second Step: Chapter One (2022): This marked their first comeback after a long break. The title track "JIKJIN" presented a more mature and charismatic image. The album also included popular B-side tracks like "U" and "DARARI." "DARARI" became especially viral worldwide, gaining popularity on social media platforms and introducing TREASURE to a wider audience.

The Second Step: Chapter Two (2022): This album continued their musical growth, featuring songs that highlight their vocal and rap abilities. It included tracks that balanced both energetic and emotional styles, further proving their versatility.

Reboot (2023): This is TREASURE’s second full-length album and represents a new era for the group. The album includes the title track "BONA BONA," which features a powerful and addictive sound. Many members participated in writing and producing the songs, showing their development as musicians. Other tracks such as "I WANT YOUR LOVE," "RUN," and "STUPID" highlight their diverse musical styles.

Overall, TREASURE’s discography continues to grow, reflecting their artistic evolution and strong connection with their fans worldwide.


Musical Style and Concept


TREASURE is widely known for their unique musical identity often referred to as the "TREASURE SOUND." Their music is characterized by energetic beats, catchy melodies, and youthful vibes that appeal to a wide audience. From the beginning of their career, they focused on creating songs that are easy to listen to while still delivering powerful performances.

In their early releases, TREASURE showcased a bright and youthful concept, often called "teen crush." Songs like "MY TREASURE" highlighted positivity, hope, and encouragement, especially for young listeners. Their cheerful concepts helped them connect emotionally with fans around the world.

As the group matured, their music style evolved into a more powerful and charismatic direction. Tracks like "JIKJIN" and "BONA BONA" show a stronger and more confident image. These songs feature heavier beats, intense choreography, and a more mature concept compared to their earlier releases.

TREASURE is also known for participating in the creation of their music. Several members are involved in songwriting, composing, and producing, which adds authenticity and personal expression to their songs. Their ability to blend different genres such as pop, hip-hop, dance, and ballad makes their music versatile and appealing.

Overall, TREASURE’s musical style continues to evolve, reflecting their growth as artists while maintaining their signature energetic and engaging sound.


Achievements and World Tour


Since their debut, TREASURE has achieved numerous milestones and gained recognition both in South Korea and internationally. They quickly established themselves as one of the most promising rookie groups in K-pop.

During their debut years, TREASURE received several "Rookie of the Year" awards from major music award shows, recognizing their strong impact in the industry. Their albums achieved high sales, with millions of copies sold worldwide, making them one of the best-selling fourth-generation boy groups.

The group has also achieved impressive digital success, with their songs gaining millions of streams and views on platforms like YouTube and Spotify. Their music videos consistently reach high view counts, showing their global popularity and strong fan support.

In terms of live performances, TREASURE successfully held their first concert tour titled "HELLO TOUR." This tour included multiple stops across Asia, where they performed in front of thousands of fans. Their performances were praised for their energy, live vocals, and strong stage presence.

Following this, they continued their global activities with the "REBOOT TOUR," which expanded to larger venues, including arena and dome concerts in Japan. Performing in dome venues is considered a major achievement for K-pop artists, highlighting their popularity and success in the Japanese market.

Through their achievements and world tours, TREASURE has proven their ability to connect with fans worldwide. Their dedication, talent, and continuous growth ensure that they remain one of the leading groups in the global K-pop industry.
